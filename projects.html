Steps to create Heads or tails coin flip game

  1. Put in images of the head of a coin, and the other side of the coin
  2. Create a select option with two choices of heads or tails to take the users choice
  3. Put in a randomizer to generate a value being 1 or 2, 1 representing heads and 2 representing tails
  4. Put in if statements to show a message if the users choice is the same as the randomizer's value
  5. Connect randommizer value to show an image of what side of the coin it is now
